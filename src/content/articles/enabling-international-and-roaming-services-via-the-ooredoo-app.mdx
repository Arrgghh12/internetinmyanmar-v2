Hey there, tech-savvy adventurers! Are you ready to stay connected while you jet off across borders? 🌍 Whether you're heading to a business meeting in Bangkok or a beach getaway in Bali, enabling **international calls** and **roaming services** with the Ooredoo app is easier than finding a good bowl of mohinga!




## Step 1: Download the Ooredoo App




If you haven't done so yet, it's time to hit the app store and grab the **Ooredoo app**. It's your ultimate guide to all things Ooredoo and will make managing your account a piece of cake.




## Step 2: Log In and Navigate to Services




Once you're in the app, log in using your credentials. If you’ve forgotten them, don’t sweat it! Just follow the prompts to reset. Now, look for the **“Services”** tab in the menu. It’s usually hard to miss — like a pup at a dog park!




## Step 3: Activating International Calls




- Tap on “International Calls”.
- Choose your desired service (don’t worry, they usually give you plenty of options).
- Select your preferred payment method (it’s Ooredoo, so you know they’ve got you covered!).
- Click “Activate” and just like that, you’re set!




## Step 4: Enabling Roaming Services




Now, let’s make sure you won’t be lost in the middle of nowhere with a phone that doesn't work!




- In the same menu, look for “Roaming Services”.
- Don’t be shy! Tap on it like you would on that last slice of pizza.
- Select the areas you’ll be traveling to and choose your preferred roaming package.
- Hit “Activate” again. Voila! You’re ready to roam!




## Double-Check Roaming Costs




Before you step onto that plane, it’s wise to double-check the roaming costs for your destination. You can find this info [here](https://www.ooredoo.com.mm/en/international-roaming/). It's better than getting a surprise bill upon return, right?
