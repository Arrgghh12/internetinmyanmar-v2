Internet Service Provider Net Core announces today a new promotion. 1 month free for any subscription to Corporate Internet starting from October 2016.

Net Core is an official brand of Spectrum Life, an ICT company in Myanmar providing end-to-end enterprise solutions. Netcore is operating in Yangon and is currently looking at expanding its services to Mandalay.
