Internet Service Provider Bluewave Networks announced last week a new promotion on its residential and corporate packages. Starting in october, any subscription will come with 1 month free Internet.
Bluewave Internet is available in Pearl Condo according to their Facebook page.
According to our sources, Bluewave service is only available in Yangon.
