Xiaomi Mi 11 is the world’s first smartphone powered by the Snapdragon 888 SoC. The new flagship smartphone was released last month in China in China. Xiaomi reported that in just 21 days, the company sold 1,000,000 (one million) **Xiaomi** **Mi** **11**.

_Xiaomi Mi 11 soon release in Myanmar_



## **Xiaomi Mi 11 Price in Myanmar**




To date, Xiaomi Mi 11 is not available in the global version but you can find it in the China version in Myanmar. The Xiaomi Mi 11 can be bought at Yangon Mobile Store for **893,000Ks (8/128GB version)** or **950,000Ks (8/256GB version)**.




The phone went on sale in China on January 1. Xiaomi has not provided details on the international availability of the Mi 11 just yet.




The Xiaomi Mi 11 ships in Horizon Blue, Frost White, and Midnight Gray colours.




## **Xiaomi Mi 11 Main Specifications**




As said at the beginning of the article, Mi11 is the very first phone powered by the latest Snapdragon 888 SoC. The chip is bundled with up to 12GB of RAM and up to 256GB of storage. Choose your model carefully because the Mi 11 doesn’t embed a microSD card slot.




The Mi 11 is powered by a heavy 4,600 mAh battery with 55W wired fast-charging support and 50W wireless charging support. It also supports 10W reverse charging.




## **Xiaomi Mi 11 Display **




Lets face it, the display is definitely the most exciting part of the Xiaomi Mi 11. The smartphone features a 6.81-inch AMOLED panel with a QHD+ resolution and a 120Hz refresh rate.




The panel on the Mi 11 also supports a 480Hz touch sampling rate with peak brightness of 1,500 nits. The Mi 11 is protected by Gorilla Glass Victus, similar to the Galaxy Note 20 Ultra.




Xiaomi has also selected a curved front panel with Goodix’s latest optical fingerprint scanner.




## **Xiaomi Mi 11 Camera **




The Xiaomi Mi 11 is furnished with a 108 MP main camera sensor with a 7P lens and f/1.85 aperture. The primary camera is bundled with a 13 MP ultrawide camera and a third 5 MP macro lens.

Connectivity-wise, the Mi 11 supports 5Gm, Wi-Fi 6E, 4G LTE, Infrared (IR), Bluetooth 5.2 and NFC. The phone also embeds stereo speakers with Harman Kardon audio.




Finally, the Xiaomi Mi 11 measures 8.06mm thick and weighs 196 grams.
