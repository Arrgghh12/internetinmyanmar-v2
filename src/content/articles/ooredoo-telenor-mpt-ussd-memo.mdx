If you feel like using a mobile operator app is just a waste of your precious smartphone resources, you are probably stuck remembering by heart the USSD codes of your favorite mobile operators. And you probably end up spending a lot of time browsing the Internet to find the relevant codes. It is incredible to see how often these questions pop-up over the Internet.

_How to check my balance on MPT?_
_How to monitor my data usage on my Telenor package?_
_Is my data plan expired?_
_Can I transfer topup to a friend on Ooredoo network?_

Internet in Myanmar wants to make your life easier! We did a little of research and came up with an all-in-one memo so you don't have to look for the codes anymore.

The memo is available in PNG and PDF version so you can download and print it easily.
