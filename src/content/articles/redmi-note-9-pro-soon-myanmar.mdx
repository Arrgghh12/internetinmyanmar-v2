Xiaomi is very busy in the news these days. Three days after [launching MIUI 12](/xiaomi-miui-12-mi10-youth/), the Chinese technology vendor launched four products in a single day on April 30, 2020. The launch included Redmi Note 9 and Note 9 Pro. Mi Note 10 Lite was also part of the launch. The last addition was a Mi Smart Standing Fan 1C.


## Redmi Note 9


Redmi Note 9 is Xiaomi's brand new vanilla model. It features a 6.53-inch IPS display with an FHD resolution and a standard 13-inch punch-hole cut-out selfie camera. The rear is equipped with a quad camera comprised of a 48 megapixel Samsung GM1 main sensor, an 8 MP ultrawide lens, and two 2MP lenses--- one for macro stills, and second one for depth.

[![Xiaomi Redmi Note 9 Myanmar Yangon Internet Mobile Price MMK Ks Smartphone](/images/redmi-note-9-pro-soon-myanmar/Myanmar-Redmi-Note-9.png)](/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Myanmar-Redmi-Note-9.png) Redmi Note 9

Redmi Note 9 is dual SIM and embeds the new MediaTek Helio G85 chipset along with an octa-core CPU. The phone is available with either 3/4GB RAM and 64/128GB storage and powered by a powerful 5,020 mAh battery with 18W fast charging.

The new Redmi Note 9 is priced at $199 for the 3GB/64GB model. The 4GB/128GB model is available for $249. Prices in Myanmar will come soon.


## **Redmi Note 9 Pro**


The Global Redmi Note 9 Pro model is identical to Note 9 Pro Max. Note 9 Pro (Global) packs a 6.67 FHD+ IPS display with a Gorilla Glass 5 protection. At the front, it is equipped with a 32 MP selfie camera. At the rear, a quad-camera set, main shooter is 64 MP, with an 8MP ultrawide lens, a 5MP macro lens and a 2MP depth sensor.

The Redmi Note 9 Pro runs on a Snapdragon 720G chipset along with an Octa-core CPU(2x2.3 GHz Kryo 465 Gold & 6x1.8 GHz Kryo 465 Silver). The handset is powered by a 5,020 mAh battery and supports wired fast charging.

[![Xiaomi Redmi Note 9 Pro Myanmar Internet Yangon Mobile Price](/images/redmi-note-9-pro-soon-myanmar/Redmi-Note-9-Pro.jpg)](/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Redmi-Note-9-Pro.jpg) Redmi Note 9 Pro

The Redmi Note 9 series will be available in 6GB/64GB and 6GB/128GB variants. The 6GB/64GB model is priced $269 while the 6GB/128GB at $299.

## **Mi Note 10 Lite**


The Xiaomi Mi Note 10 lite holds the premium features of Mi Note 10 beside few tweaks. The rear camera design has only four cameras instead of five for Mi Note 10. It keeps the 6.4-inch Full HD+ display with curved edges.

The quad-camera is equipped of a 64-MPSony IMX686 sensor-based main camera along with an 8-MP wide-angle camera, a 2-MP macro camera, and a 5-MP depth camera. It does not have the 108 MP camera of the standard Note 10 but a 32MP selfie camera.

[![Xiaomi Note 10 Lite Myanmar Yangon Internet Mobile Price MMK Ks Smartphone](/images/redmi-note-9-pro-soon-myanmar/mi-note-10-lite-myanmar.jpg)](/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/mi-note-10-lite-myanmar.jpg) Mi Note 10 Lite

The core processor is a Snapdragon 730G that is paired with up to 8GB RAM and 128GB of internal storage. The 5260mAh battery promise to deliver up to two days on a single charge. Mi Note 10 Lite also supports fast charging (30W).

Mi Note 10 Lite is available in 6GB/64GB and 6GB/128GB variants. The 128 GB variant is available at $399. Price in Myanmar will follow.
