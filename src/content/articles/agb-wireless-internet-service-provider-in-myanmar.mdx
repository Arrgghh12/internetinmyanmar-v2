[AGB Communication Co., Ltd.](https://www.facebook.com/AGB.Communication.Co.Ltd) is a trading company, importing and exporting a variety of products into and from Myanmar. AGB receives in 2015 a NFS-I license authorizing them to deliver Internet services all over Myanmar primarily focusing on Yangon initially but expanding to various areas in Myanmar.

AGB core business being IT, it naturally focuses on providing Internet services to corporate customers but residential plans are available as well.

Technology-wise, AGB claims to use both Wireless (WiFi) and Fiber (FTTB FTTH) to serve its customers.

In Yangon, AGB wireless service is available in the locations below:


- FMI City (Hlaing Thar Yar)
- Nawaddy Garden Housing (Hlaing Thar Yar)
- Hlaing Thar Yar Industrial Zone (1,2,3,4)
- Pan Hlaing Real Estate
- Mayangone Township
- Ahlone Township
- Sanchaung Chaung Township (Myaynigone)
- Bahan Township (Golden Valley)
