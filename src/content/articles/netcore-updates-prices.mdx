> ⚠️ First published in 2017. Some figures may be outdated.

Myanmar Internet Service Provider Net Core has recently updated its pricelist for residential and corporate customers.

The new pricelist can be found on NetCore [website](https://netcoreinternet.com/) and below.

[![internet myanmar netcore](/images/netcore-updates-prices/netcore-myanmar.png)](/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/netcore-myanmar.png)

Residential plans see a significant drop and are now aligned with Bluewave home plans.


### Subsidized installation fees


Installation fees which are a big barrier to entry for residential customers have been reviewed as well and become a big selling point:

Netcore home customers will be charged 60,000 MMK one time and then 10,000Ks each month during 6 months.


### Only available in Yangon


As of today, NetCore broadband service is only available in Yangon but the Myanmar ISP has plans to bring its service to Mandalay in the coming year.
