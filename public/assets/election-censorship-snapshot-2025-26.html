Key Finding: Myanmar's OONI anomaly rate showed no election-day spike on December 28, January 11, or January 25. The junta did not need emergency blackouts — a pre-existing censorship infrastructure blocking 60–90% of independent media, social platforms, and VPN services made acute shutdown actions unnecessary. This is ambient censorship: structural, continuous, and invisible to tools calibrated for acute disruption events.
